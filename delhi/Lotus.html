About the Lotus Temple Entry Ticket, Delhi:
Experience the serenity and tranquility of the Lotus Temple, a symbol of peace and unity. Designed by Iranian architect Fariborz Sahba, the Lotus Temple is known for its breathtaking architecture, which is inspired by the shape of a lotus flower. The temple welcomes people of all faiths and beliefs, offering them a place for silent prayer and meditation. Explore the beautiful gardens and serene surroundings of the Lotus Temple complex, which provide a peaceful retreat from the hustle and bustle of the city. Participate in prayers and meditation sessions to experience the spiritual ambiance of the temple. How to Reach:
Nearest Metro Station: Kalkaji Mandir Metro Station is the nearest metro station located on the Violet Line of the Delhi Metro. It is located within walking distance from the Lotus Temple.
Nearest Bus Stop: Kalkaji Extension Bus Stop is the nearest bus stop located near the temple. You can hire a taxi or use public transportation to reach the Lotus Temple from the bus stop.
Nearest Railway Station: Hazrat Nizamuddin Railway Station is the nearest railway station located at a distance of approximately 15 km from the Lotus Temple. You can hire a taxi or use public transportation to reach the temple from the railway station.
