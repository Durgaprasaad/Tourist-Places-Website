How to Reach:
Nearest Airport: Indira Gandhi International Airport is the nearest airport located at a distance of approximately 18 km from the Red Fort. You can hire a taxi or use public transportation to reach the fort from the airport.
Nearest Metro Station: Chandni Chowk Metro Station is the nearest metro station located on the Yellow Line of the Delhi Metro. It is located within walking distance from the Red Fort.
Nearest Railway Station: New Delhi Railway Station is the nearest railway station located at a distance of approximately 6 km from the Red Fort. You can hire a taxi or use public transportation to reach the fort from the railway station.
