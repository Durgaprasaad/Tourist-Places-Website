About the Nehru Zoological Park Entry Ticket, Hyderabad:
Explore the fascinating world of wildlife at Nehru Zoological Park, one of the largest zoos in Asia. Spread over 380 acres, the zoo is home to over 1,500 species of animals, birds, and reptiles from around the world. Enjoy a leisurely walk through the park and encounter a diverse range of wildlife, including tigers, lions, elephants, giraffes, and more. Don't miss the safari ride, where you can witness animals roaming freely in naturalistic habitats. With its informative exhibits, interactive experiences, and conservation efforts, Nehru Zoological Park offers an unforgettable wildlife experience for visitors of all ages.

How to Reach:
Nearest Airport: Rajiv Gandhi International Airport is the nearest airport located at a distance of 22.5 km from the zoo. You can hire a taxi from the airport to reach the zoo.
Nearest Bus Stand: Zoo Park Main Gate Bus Stop is the nearest bus stand located at a distance of 1.2 km from the zoo. You can hire a taxi from the bus stand to reach the zoo.
Nearest Railway Station: Hyderabad Deccan Railway Station is the nearest railway station located at a distance of 7.5 km from the zoo. You can hire a taxi from the railway station to reach the zoo.
