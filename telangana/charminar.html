About the Charminar Entry Ticket, Hyderabad:
Visit Charminar, an iconic landmark in Hyderabad, often called the 'Arc de Triomphe of the East.' Its name, Charminar, originates from Urdu, signifying 'four turrets' (char-four, minar-turret). Constructed in 1591 CE, this monument has four towering 95-foot minarets that are sure to grab your attention. Admire the gorgeous Islamic-style architecture of the minar and go to the highest point of the attraction for amazing views of the city. Explore the bustling markets around Charminar and savor delicious street food. Book entry tickets to Charminar and see the four grand arches that stand tall like a grand gateway to the city.
